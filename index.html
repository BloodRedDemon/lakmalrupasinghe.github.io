Daoyuan Wu

Research interests: smartphone security, system security, mobile network, and mobile system.
I enjoy performing security analysis and building real systems.
I in particular like discovering new security problems and conducting large-scale measurement evaluation.
I am now a first-year PhD student at School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University (SMU).
My advisors are Prof. Debin Gao and Prof. Robert H. Deng. I also work with Prof. Rocky K. C. Chang on mobile network measurement.
