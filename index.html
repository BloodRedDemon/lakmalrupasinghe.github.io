I am a second-year PhD student at School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University (SMU).

I am working closely with my two PhD advisors, Prof. Debin Gao and Prof. Robert H. Deng, on a number of interesting security projects.

At SMU, I also get chance to collaborate with Prof. Yingjiu Li on Android security, and with Prof. David Lo on software engineering.

My previous MPhil advisor at HK PolyU was Prof. Rocky K. C. Chang, with whom I am currently working on mobile network measurement.


Research interests: Smartphone and Mobile Security; Crowdsourcing and Network Measurement.


I am leading the design and implementation of two crowdsourcing projects. Please consider to try them for fun and knowledge:

  • MopEye measures the per-app network speed, and many more (capture packets, change DNS).

  • NetMon scans your 3G/4G/WiFi networks, and monitors open ports in your device.
